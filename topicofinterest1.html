Who I am...

I strive to make meaning in a world of unintelligible gobbledigook.
Sometimes I tell stories, sometimes I make lists. Our world is flooded with information. I'm here to make sense out of it.

Background...

My path to technical communication was not the usual one (if there even is such a path). I studied Music and Sound Recording Technology, interned in a recording studio, worked in technical support, taught English overseas, managed an Alamo Drafthouse, tended many bars and waited many tables. Each experience taugh me valuable lessons about people and communication.

It was my time as a bookseller (at the best indie bookstore ever... Austin's BookPeople) that finally put me on the path to the Graduate College at Texas State University and, evenutually, to writing documentation for a living.
